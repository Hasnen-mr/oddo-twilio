Twilio Connector – Calling, SMS and Call Logs

Use your own Twilio account from Odoo to send SMS, view call logs, and initiate click-to-call.

Features

  • Configuration – Store your Twilio Account SID and Auth Token per company. Test the connection with one click.
  • Send SMS – Compose and send SMS from Odoo using your Twilio phone numbers.
  • SMS History – Fetch and view recent SMS messages from Twilio on demand.
  • Call Logs – Fetch and view call logs from Twilio with optional recording links.
  • Click-to-call – Initiate outbound calls: your phone rings first, then the contact is dialed. Requires your Odoo instance to be reachable by Twilio (public URL) for the dial TwiML.

Requirements

You need a Twilio account (twilio.com). There is no intermediary server: the app uses your credentials to talk directly to the Twilio API. Your data is sent to Twilio according to their services and privacy policy.

Installation

Copy the module into your Odoo addons path and install it from the Apps menu. Configure Twilio under the Twilio menu (Configuration) with your Account SID and Auth Token.
