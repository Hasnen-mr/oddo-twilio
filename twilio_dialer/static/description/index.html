Twilio Dialer - Free Calling, SMS & Call Logs for Odoo

Make and receive calls, send SMS, and view Twilio logs directly within your Odoo – 100% no middle server, secure, and privacy-first. Use your own Twilio account to handle all business communications from a single, unified Odoo interface.

Why Choose Twilio Dialer for Odoo?

  • 100% Free for Odoo Community & Enterprise - Pay only Twilio usage, no module subscriptions.
  • Privacy First - Credentials saved only to your Odoo instance, never sent to third-party servers.
  • No Tracking or Analytics - Your data and communication history stay private within Odoo and Twilio.

Complete Twilio Communication Suite for Odoo

  • ✅ Make outgoing voice calls: Click-to-call any number using your Twilio phone numbers.
  • ✅ Receive incoming calls: Twilio can reach you through your browser or device.
  • ✅ Send SMS/text messages: Compose and send directly from Odoo CRM or Contacts.
  • ✅ View SMS and call history: Complete logs fetched from Twilio, including messages and calls with details and optional recordings.
  • ✅ Track all communication: See who called, when, and full message details.
  • ✅ Bulk SMS: Reach multiple customers at once if enabled in your Twilio account.

Zero Additional Infrastructure

No backend required beyond Odoo. No external servers or services. Simply enter your Twilio Account SID, Auth Token, and phone number in Odoo configuration to enable full calling and SMS features.

Key Features

  • WebRTC-based voice calling: Clear, reliable browser calls via Twilio.
  • Click-to-call from any contact or lead: Instantly start calls from Odoo forms and lists.
  • Receive calls if public URL enabled: Use Odoo’s web.base.url for incoming call forwarding.
  • Advanced SMS messaging: Send, receive, and thread messages inside Odoo.
  • Complete logs & reporting: Browse, filter, and export call/SMS history for compliance and record-keeping.
  • Security: Credentials and logs stored only in your Odoo/PostgreSQL database; nothing leaves your infrastructure except calls to Twilio's API.

How It Works

  1. Install the module in your Odoo instance (18.0 or 17.0 compatible).
  2. Navigate to Twilio → Configuration, enter your Twilio Account SID, Auth Token, and phone number.
  3. Start making calls, sending SMS, and reviewing call/message logs all from within Odoo – no browser extension needed.
  4. Use standard Odoo user rights to manage access to SMS and call features.

Availability

Odoo.sh and self-hosted (on-premise): Yes. Install the module from the Apps menu or from the Odoo Apps Store.

Odoo Online (SaaS): Not available. Odoo Online does not allow installation of third-party modules that contain Python code. To use this app you need Odoo.sh or a self-hosted Odoo instance.

Installation

  1. Copy the twilio_dialer folder into your Odoo addons path.
  2. Update the Apps list and install "Twilio Dialer – Calling, SMS and Call Logs".
  3. Go to the Twilio menu in Odoo and configure your Twilio credentials.
